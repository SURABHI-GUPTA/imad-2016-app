Billionaire Warren Buffett invests in 4 US airlines

NEW YORK: Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway Inc on Monday said it has bought shares in the four biggest US airlines: American Airlines Group Inc, Delta Air Lines Inc, Southwest Airlines Co and United Continental Holdings Inc. The investments mark an unexpected reversal for Berkshire, which has avoided the airline sector for nearly two decades after a troubled investment in the former US Air Group, a forerunner to American.They also expand Berkshire's bet on the US economy, and in particular transportation. Berkshire already owns the BNSF railroad and the NetJets luxury plane unit, and in January paid $32.1 billion for aircraft parts maker Precision Castparts.
